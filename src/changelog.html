Several features were added on the 1.0.4, here you have the most relevant ones:

Added changelog, yep, pretty obvious as you're now seeing it. But we must tell you as that's his purpose.

More friendly UI to load music, you can drop a folder to scan music from there. Better and easier!

You can resize the window to an smaller width, the UI will change and some options won't be available.

Player improvements:

  • Shuffle now will reproduce the previous shuffle song.
  • If the song has played more than 3 seconds changing to the previous song will restart the active one.
  • You can add/change the album art image! Right click a song and press over «change album art», this will open a dialog to select an existing image.

Settings window: what will be an app without his settings? So here you have it. You can open it pressing Ctrl + O or right clicking on a song and pressing «settings». We are currently building it so there're not many things done yet. Here you will be able to change the theme, hotkeys and some other parameters.
